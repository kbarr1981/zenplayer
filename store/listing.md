### Description (4000 chars max)
Zen MP3 Player is a focused MP3 player for the music you already own.

No accounts. No suggestions. No social feed. Load your own audio files and let them play — through a clean dark interface, a soft amber visualizer, and an 8-band parametric equalizer that gives you full control of the sound.

KEY FEATURES
• 8-band equalizer (60 Hz — 12 kHz) with fine ±12 dB control on every band
• Six built-in presets — Flat, Bass, Vocal, Rock, Jazz, Classical
• Live audio visualizer that responds to what's playing
• Multi-track playlist — load a folder, queue a session
• Volume, scrub, skip, repeat — all the controls, none of the noise
• Native standalone player — runs full-screen, no chrome, no distractions
• Dark theme tuned for evening listening
• Works with any audio file your device supports (MP3, AAC, FLAC, WAV, M4A)

A QUIET DESIGN
Zen MP3 Player is built for the moment you actually want to hear the music — not be sold to. The aesthetic is intentionally restrained: warm gold and brown over deep black, Japanese serif type, a still zen garden underneath the controls. It's a player that earns its place on your home screen.

ABOUT THE EQ
The eight bands cover the full audible range with the curves that matter — sub-bass thump, low-mid warmth, vocal presence, sibilance, air. Drag any band, save the result. The presets are starting points, not destinations.

PRIVACY
Zen MP3 Player does not collect data. Your music stays on your device. No tracking, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs.

## Submission Notes

**iOS minimum-functionality risk (guideline 4.2):** WebView wrappers are sometimes
rejected for being "just a website." To pass review, emphasize in the App Review
notes that the EQ, visualizer, and offline playback work without an internet
connection — the app is functional standalone, not a website wrapper.

**Audio category compliance:** if/when background playback is added, declare
the `audio` background mode in Info.plist and update screenshots to show
lock-screen now-playing controls.
